OUR STORY

More Than Just a Haircut

Founded in 2015 by master barber James Wilson, Empire Fades has grown from a small chair in the heart of Liverpool to the city's most sought-after barbershop for precision fades and modern men's grooming.

What started as a passion for the craft has become a destination for men who appreciate quality, attention to detail, and an atmosphere that blends urban culture with luxury service.

Our barbers are trained in the latest techniques from London to Los Angeles, bringing world-class skills to the streets of Liverpool. We don't just cut hair - we craft confidence.
