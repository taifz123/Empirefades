Our Story

More Than A Barbershop — It's A Culture

Founded in 2015 by master barber James Wilson, Empire Fades has evolved from a single chair in Liverpool to a destination grooming house known for precision fades and modern techniques inspired by the global scene.

Every appointment blends craftsmanship with community. From curated playlists to conversations that matter, Empire Fades is where sharp cuts and authentic connection meet.
